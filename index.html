About Jeffrey

Jeffrey Seligson is a seasoned West County real estate agent with expertise spanning residential, multi-unit, and commercial properties. He holds certifications as an EcoBroker and Seniors Real Estate Specialist, with particular knowledge of well and septic systems and sustainable building practices.

Throughout his 25+-year career in local real estate, Seligson has earned numerous accolades including Leading Edge Society and President's Club. Seven years running, Jeffrey won Best Sonoma County Agent in the North Bay Bohemian's 2007-2013 Reader's Polls.

A 39-year Bay Area resident and 36-year Sebastopol resident, Jeffrey leverages a unique and proactive pre-listing strategy. He has a professional network of contractors, energy specialists, and solar installers, and extensive local expertise in West Sonoma County and its environs. His practice includes pre-listing preparation, green-building perspectives, and ease in handling the most complex transactions or most problematic property situations.

Jeffrey is a Team of One, so you always know who is there for you, immediately on the job to represent your best interest!
