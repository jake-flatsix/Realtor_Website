About Jeffrey

Jeffrey Seligson is a seasoned West County real estate agent with expertise spanning residential, multi-unit, and commercial properties. He holds certifications as an EcoBroker and Seniors Real Estate Specialist, with particular knowledge of well and septic systems and sustainable building practices.

Throughout his 21-year career in local real estate, Seligson earned numerous accolades including Leading Edge Society and President's Club recognition. Seven years running, he won Best Sonoma County Agent in the North Bay Bohemian's 2007-2013 Reader's Polls.

A 39-year Bay Area resident and 36-year Sebastopol resident, he leverages extensive local expertise and a professional network of contractors, energy specialists, and solar installers. His practice includes pre-listing preparation, construction consultation, and assistance with complex transactions.
